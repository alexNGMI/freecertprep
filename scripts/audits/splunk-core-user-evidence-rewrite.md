## Fidelity decision

The complete learning pool retains matching and ordering drills because they help learners connect concepts and workflows. The official-style 60-question simulator excludes those drill formats and selects only single-choice and multiple-response items.

## Remaining editorial risk

The bank intentionally revisits high-value entry-level concepts across different evidence sets. Tests now prevent exact duplicates and require at least 500 normalized structures, but future review should continue replacing weaker templates with screenshots, richer result tables, and more command-specific distractor analysis.
