# Network+ and Security+ Learning Loop Audit

Date: June 9, 2026

## Scope

- CompTIA Network+ N10-009
- CompTIA Security+ SY0-701
- CCNA explicitly excluded and left parked

## Official source spine

- CompTIA Network+ certification page and N10-009 exam objectives
- CompTIA Security+ certification page and SY0-701 exam objectives
- CompTIA performance-based question guidance

## Objective coverage

| Bank | Questions | Official objectives | Practical items | Conservative fallback |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Network+ | 760 | 27 | 32 | 71 / 760 (9.3%) |
| Security+ | 760 | 28 | 33 | 102 / 760 (13.4%) |

Every objective has at least three questions and at least one classified topic family. Every question carries `objectiveId`, `objectiveTitle`, and `conceptId`. The audit fails if fallback classification exceeds 20%, metadata is missing, an objective/domain mapping is invalid, or a practical explanation is shorter than 140 characters. The audit deliberately does not manufacture concept diversity from question IDs; thin classified families remain visible for future editorial expansion.

## Learner value

- Dashboards show objective accuracy separately from question coverage.
- Learners can open a ten-question session for one numbered objective.
- Recent misses are available as a dedicated repair queue.
- Due review uses question accuracy and elapsed time to schedule retention practice.
- Question and review surfaces display the objective being practiced.
- Practice results identify up to three objectives responsible for the learner's misses.

## Readiness decision

Network+ and Security+ remain **B+**. The objective learning loop materially improves usefulness and diagnostic precision. The remaining gap is interaction depth: the platform approximates proprietary CompTIA PBQs and should continue adding richer cable, topology, firewall, log, and multi-artifact tasks without claiming exact vendor scoring.
