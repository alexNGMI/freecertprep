## Production Gate

- 750 total questions.
- Domain counts match the closest whole-question split for Cisco 200-301 v2.0.
- Simulation types cover CLI output, topology interpretation, configuration repair, and subnetting drills.
- Stems are duplicate-free.
- Explanations meet the minimum review-depth gate.
- Registry publishes `ccna-200-301` and exposes it in the full catalog.
- Networking path routes Network+ or CCST Networking into CCNA.
- Data Center Technician path routes Server+ to Schneider DCCA to CCNA.

## Ongoing Quality Notes

CCNA should keep receiving deeper manual audits because it is the first advanced simulation-backed cert. Future improvements should add more multi-command troubleshooting evidence, more varied topology diagrams, and more applied OSPF, VLAN, NAT/PAT, ACL, wireless, AI operations, and management scenarios.
