# Course-Companion Usefulness Audit

Review date: June 17, 2026

## Purpose

This audit answers one practical question:

Can a learner combine freecertprep with a free YouTube playlist, vendor learning path, or low-cost course and realistically reach the intended outcome?

The answer is yes for the live catalog, with one important boundary: freecertprep is a readiness coach and practice system, not a full lecture course, not a hands-on lab platform, and not a claim to reproduce confidential vendor scoring.

## Bottom Line

freecertprep is strongest when paired with a primary instructional course:

- The course teaches the concepts.
- freecertprep diagnoses weak areas.
- The mastery map turns practice evidence into a study plan.
- Case practice converts recall into scenario reasoning.
- Exam debriefs tell the learner what to repair next.

This makes the product more valuable than a plain question bank. It should be positioned as a course companion plus readiness simulator.

## Path-Level Judgment

### Entry IT

A+ is the strongest beginner offering. With Professor Messer or an equivalent course, the app can realistically help a learner move from concept exposure to exam readiness. The missing piece is real hands-on hardware, OS, printer, mobile, and support workflow practice.

Recommended outcome language: "Use this with a course and some hands-on practice to prepare for A+."

### Networking

Network+ is the flagship networking readiness product. It is stronger than CCST for broad vendor-neutral career prep. CCST is useful for learners who specifically want a Cisco-first entry step toward CCNA, but it should not replace Network+ in the general networking path.

Recommended outcome language: "Use Network+ for broad networking readiness; use CCST if your target is Cisco-first."

### Cybersecurity

Security+ plus Splunk is a coherent career path. Security+ supplies the recognized baseline. Splunk adds applied SOC/search fluency. Together with a course, the pair can help a learner prepare for entry cybersecurity interviews better than either alone.

Recommended outcome language: "Security+ gets you the baseline vocabulary and exam credential; Splunk helps you show tool fluency."

### Cloud

The cloud path is sensible as CLF-C02 -> SAA-C03 -> Terraform. CLF-C02 is optional and best for learners new to AWS. SAA-C03 is the strongest role-facing credential in the path. Terraform adds a deployable infrastructure skill that can make the resume more practical.

Recommended outcome language: "Skip CLF-C02 if AWS basics are already comfortable; use SAA and Terraform as the job-facing core."

## What The App Provides That Courses Usually Do Not

- A diagnostic that creates a baseline without immediate feedback.
- Strong / Developing / Weak / Not measured mastery states.
- A study plan generated from the learner's actual practice evidence.
- Recent-miss and weak-area recycling through Smart Practice.
- Exam debriefs that point to the next repair target.
- Case practice that converts concepts into applied decisions.
- Local-first progress with no account requirement.

## What Courses Still Provide That The App Does Not

- Full concept teaching from zero.
- Instructor sequencing and explanations.
- Hands-on labs, cloud consoles, terminals, or real Splunk search work.
- Vendor-specific UI familiarity.
- Human judgment on when to book an exam.
- Real hardware, OS, cloud, networking, or SOC environment practice.

## Explicit Exclusions

This audit excludes AZ-900, Google Cloud Digital Leader, CCNA, NVIDIA AI Infrastructure and Operations, NVIDIA Generative AI LLMs, Server+, Linux+, Schneider DCCA, and Real Estate.

Those modules are either Coming Soon, parked for future release alignment, hidden, or outside the current IT course-companion strategy.

## Roadmap Implications

The next step is not a larger catalog.

The highest-value roadmap items are:

1. Add local data durability: completed-result recovery, storage failure warnings, compact history, and full-study backup.
2. Add one `verify:quality` command for lint, tests, build, dependency audit, and cert-specific audits.
3. Add browser smoke coverage for the learner loop.
4. Add a CLF-C02-specific quality gate.
5. Add a dedicated SAA audit command if SAA remains a flagship cloud module.
6. Keep Splunk and Terraform in maintenance mode with periodic manual sampling.
7. Add external companion links in a structured way, but do not turn the app into a course directory.

## Board-Level Recommendation

Position freecertprep as a free course companion and readiness coach for the live IT catalog.

The honest promise is:

"Use a course to learn. Use freecertprep to find weak spots, practice exam-shaped questions, repair gaps, and decide whether you are ready."

That is strong, defensible, and differentiated.
